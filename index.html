Loupyboy – Creating interactive media since 2010
I'm Loup Dauty, mostly known as Loupyboy or Kami Sensei on the web. I make music, video, websites, games, artworks... I create things.
My crafts
I like to create a lot of different things — be it with friends or alone (but mostly with friends), and this should be a good place to start from if you're wondering what I'm doing with my life.
Creating Music
I'm composing, arranging and performing most of my tracks by myself, and if you want to give some of them a go, this could be the place to look into.
I use Ableton Live, Maschine, a ton of VSTs and custom samples.
Creating Games
I design levels after levels, maps after maps, lines of code after lines of code, models after models to make neat little (or big) games! You should be able to see some of my projects here.
Mostly Unity stuff.
Creating Stories
I like to write stories. Big stories, small stories. Almost always for games. If you've ever wondered what those stories may sound like, you can try there?
Tools used are Articy:Draft, a pen & paper, Libre Office, and my mind I guess.
Creating 2D
Sometimes, I just want to chill and doodle stuff on a piece of paper. Know this feeling? You can check out my artworks, logos and album covers here.
Tools of the trade: Clip Studio Paint EX, Paint.NET and my UGEE!
Creating Websites
How would we showcase things we create without a good old website, right? I make those too. Here's a good example.
Mostly JS and HTML5 though, but I can handle PHP.
Creating 3D
Two-dimensional content is good, but 3D's simply better! You'd be able to search for stuff there.
I mainly use Sketchup to quickly get things (well) done.
